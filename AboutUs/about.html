About Amit Kapoor

Founder of Watercolour Society of India (WSI).
Vice President of International Watercolor Society Global (IWS).
President of International Watercolor Society India. (IWS-INDIA).
Brand Ambassador of Mejillo Mision Gold Colors.
Signature Brushes of world’s Finest Brand “ESCODA” from Barcelona, Spain.
Founder & Principal of Anitoons The School of Art & Animation, a division of Amarnath Educational Trust.
Editor of International Watercolor Society Magazine.
Curator & Organizer of International Watercolor Society India Biennale, India.
Curator & Organizer of 1st OLYMPIART 2019 World’s biggest Watercolor Festival.

Mr. Amit Kapoor is a leading master of watercolor medium in India. He has been recognized with top international honors for his paintings and his art graces the walls of many private and corporate collectors. He is in constant demand for solo exhibitions, workshops, and instruction. He has travelled more than 27 countries. His art captures the very soul of his subjects with passion. His exceptional drawing skills and extraordinary abilities in art make him proficient in any medium. His subjects include a lot of compositional works where the lovely play of light, shadow, and perspectives are the main traits. He is also the co-founder and Principal of Anitoons The School Of Art And Animation. He is a guest lecturer at many prestigious Art colleges like the College of Art, Delhi, Apeejay Institute of Design, Jamia Millia Islamia (Mass Communication department), Meerabai Institute of Technology. He was also on the jury panel of Jamia Millia Islamia (Mass Communication department), in the jury panel of Jaipur Animation Film Festival (JAFF). He has been interviewed by several International influential media and magazines and has a high reputation for professional production and inspired leadership. Recently, in 2013, he also launched his first book on the watercolor medium, “Amit Kapoor: LANDSCAPES IN WATERCOLOR“. He is the only Indian Watercolor Artist to get the Silver Star Award from the National Watercolor Society, USA. He is the only Indian Artist invited for a live demonstration by the world’s biggest Art Channel “SAA,” UK. Born at New Delhi in 1975. Passed BFA Applied Art, College of Art, New Delhi in 1998. Passed MA Painting, Jiwaji University, Gwalior in 2004.
